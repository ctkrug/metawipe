metawipe

100% in-browser · nothing uploads

Three steps. Zero uploads.

  1. 01

    Drop a photo

    Drag a JPEG onto the light-table, or browse for one. It's read with FileReader — the bytes never leave this tab. Pull your network cable; it still works.

  2. 02

    See the leak

    A from-scratch EXIF/TIFF/GPS parser surfaces every hidden field, pinned on the image. Location and identity leaks glow amber — including a map link to the exact spot your photo pins.

  3. 03

    Wipe it, losslessly

    One click drops the metadata segments and hands back a clean JPEG. The compressed pixels are copied byte-for-byte — no re-encode, no quality loss, just the photo minus its dossier.

Every "remove EXIF online" site asks you to upload the exact coordinates you're trying to hide. Metawipe never does. Parsing and stripping happen entirely on your device — open the network tab and watch it stay silent.
